# FORTUNE DUEL — Instruction booklet

*How the booth picks a winner after your reading and the spirit’s curse.*

---

## OBJECTIVE

Earn **more favor** than the spirit. Each card hides a **theme** (Greed, Vanity, Chaos, Power), a **moral** sticker (Good, Neutral, Bad), and a **vignette** (the funny caption on the card). The booth scores **your words** for theme weave, **loose echo of each vignette**, and **fit to the customer’s wealth** (rich or poor — look at who walked in).

---

## VICTORY CONDITIONS

| Condition | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| **Your favor > spirit’s favor** | You win. |
| **Tie score** | **You win** (house rule). |
| **Magic meter at FULL** | **You win instantly** — spirit text is ignored. |

---

## YOUR FAVOR — what adds to your score

1. **Magic meter** — Up to **+50** favor; **FULL** = automatic win (see below).
2. **Good cards** — **+10** each (Neutral = **0** here).
3. **Crowd** — More **Good** than **Bad** cards in the draw → **+15** (Neutrals not counted). More Bad than Good → those **+15** go to the **spirit**. Tie or no Good/Bad to compare → **0**.
4. **All Neutral + hope** — Every card **Neutral** *and* **≥2** different **Hope** words (list below) → **+4**. Any Good/Bad card cancels this.
5. **Theme echo** — Cards **1 → 2 → 3** in order: words from **that card’s** theme list only. Per card: **0 / 3 / 5 / 8** favor for **0 / 1 / 2 / 3+** different list words (**max 24** total).
6. **Vignette echo** — Per card, **loose** keywords from that card’s caption (e.g. *rat*, *cheese*, *deep*, *jump* — not pasted verbatim). Per card: **0 / 2 / 4 / 6** for **0 / 1 / 2 / 3+** hits (**max 18** total).
7. **Customer wealth fit** — Speak to whether the customer is **wealthy** or **poor** (see lists below). **0–15** favor: `4 × (good wealth words) − 3 × (wrong-station words)`, clamped.
8. **Tone** — Hope vs harsh words in **your** text only → **0–20** favor (formula below).

**Spirit-only from cards:** **+10** per **Bad** card (Neutral = 0).

**Spirit from words:** same **theme**, **vignette echo**, **wealth fit**, and **tone** rules on **its** curse (tone inverted: dread helps it, soft words hurt it).

## THEME VOCABULARY *(use the right list per card, left to right)*

**Greed:** greed, hunger, feast, feasting, glutton, gold, coin, coins, wealth, covet, avarice, devour, banquet, meal, meals  

**Vanity:** vanity, mirror, pride, ego, beauty, appearance, image, vain, looks, surface, reflection, mask  

**Chaos:** chaos, disorder, storm, wild, spiral, chance, fray, tumble, mayhem, wreck, shatter, snap  

**Power:** power, control, throne, crown, rule, dominion, command, chain, weight, heavy, authority, grip, reign, burden, decree, yoke, rank  

**PRO TIP:** ~**two** hits per card is efficient; extra words barely pay off. **Vanity** words on a **Chaos** card score **nothing** for that card.

---

## VIGNETTE ECHO *(loose imagery from each card caption)*

- Do **not** paste the card title. **Echo** nouns and verbs from the vignette in your own sentence.
- Example caption: *Rat jumping into a deep-fry for a piece of cheese* → words like **rat**, **jump**, **deep**, **fry**, **cheese** count if they appear in **your** reading for **that card’s slot** (whole words only).
- Per card: **0 / 2 / 4 / 6** for **0 / 1 / 2 / 3+** keyword hits.

---

## CUSTOMER WEALTH *(who walked in — rich or poor)*

**When the customer is wealthy**, favor words like: wealthy, wealth, affluent, luxury, silk, velvet, gilded, gold, estate, heir, patron, prosper, fortune, coins, inheritance, carriage, banquet, refined, privilege, comfort, lavish  

**Wealthy mismatch** (treats them like a beggar): rags, penniless, destitute, beggar, barrel, scraping, hollow belly, thin purse, starving, homeless  

**When the customer is poor**, favor words like: humble, scrape, scraping, thrift, worn, weary, bread, rent, modest, need, hunger, penny, patch, dignity, survival, thin, frayed, barrel, patched, lean, threadbare, mend  

**Poor mismatch** (flatters with luxury fiction): yacht, gilded, heir, estate, velvet, monocle, banquet, silk, limousine, trust fund, caviar, inheritance, carriage, lavish, affluent  

**Formula:** `4 × (fit words) − 3 × (mismatch words)` → **clamped 0–15**.

---

## TONE *(gentle vs cruel words)*

Each **different** list word counts **once**. Whole words only; capitals ignored. Phrases must appear exact: **walk lightly**, **hunger for**.

**You:** `5 × (hope words) − 4 × (harsh words)` → **clamped 0–20**.  
**Spirit:** `5 × (harsh words) − 4 × (hope words)` on **its** text → **clamped 0–20**.

**Hope:** hope, prosper, peace, heal, light, bless, blessing, kindness, better, grow, strength, courage, guide, balance, agency, walk lightly, navigate, uplift, safe, steady, renew, care, warmth, together, support, free, freed, fortune, forgive, forgiveness, release, relief  

**Harsh:** ruin, doom, curse, decay, rot, wither, shame, dread, corrupt, hollow, trap, fall, die, death, devour, consume, desolate, shatter, bleak, hunger for, bones, darkness, succumb, excess, corruption  

**PRO TIP:** Stack **hope**; keep **harsh** words out of **your** mouth even when naming dark themes.

---

## MAGIC METER

Some tents use a **short dial**, some a **long** one—**FULL** is always max favor.

- **Below FULL:** up to **+50** favor, scales with the dial.  
- **FULL:** **Instant win** for the teller.

---

## QUICK START *(before you submit)*

1. Glance morals — Good stack = tailwind; Bad stack = uphill; all Neutral = **speech** carries the round (**+4** if you hit **2** Hope words).  
2. Glance meter — **FULL?** Stop worrying; you already won.  
3. Note **rich or poor** customer — weave their station into the reading.  
4. Line up **three** theme echoes — one batch of words per card, **in draw order**.  
5. **Echo each vignette loosely** — image and appetite, not the caption pasted.  
6. Win the tone fight — **hope** up, **harsh** down on your side.
